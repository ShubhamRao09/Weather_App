About Our Weather App

Welcome to our Weather App! We provide real-time weather data for cities around the world, fetched through the OpenWeather API. With this app, you can search for any city and view detailed weather information such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and much more.

The app is powered by the OpenWeather API, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information when you search for a city. Our goal is to provide you with all the weather details you need in a simple and easy-to-use interface.

Key Features:
  • Real-time weather data for cities worldwide
  • Search for weather by city name
  • Detailed weather info (temperature, humidity, wind speed, etc.)
  • Weather icons and descriptions based on current conditions
  • Clean and user-friendly design

Thank you for using our Weather App! We hope it helps you stay informed about the weather wherever you go.
